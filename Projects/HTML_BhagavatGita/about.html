About the Bhagavad Gita

An introduction to the historical context, structure, and core teachings.

Overview

The Bhagavad Gita is a 700-verse scripture that is part of the Indian epic Mahabharata. It takes the form of a dialogue between Prince Arjuna and Krishna.

Structure

18 chapters covering duty, devotion, knowledge, meditation, and liberation.

How to read

Read sequentially, or jump to chapters and verses of interest. Use multiple translations and commentaries.
